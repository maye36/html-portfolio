The Recommended Movies/Series According to Mimay 2023

My top 3 movies/series.

Silenced

Silenced (Korean: 도가니; English: "The Crucible") is a 2011 South Korean crime drama film based on the novel The Crucible by Gong Ji-young. The film depicts the story of a school for the hearing-impared where young deaf students were sexually assaulted by the faculty members This movie encapsulates how society has failed to help the marginalized such as people with disability. Moreover, this questions the effectivity of the systems placed by the government to support the need. One of the most unnerving but intriging film that I have watched this year

Promising Young Woman

This was recommended by the youtubers "Cinema Therapy". It is about a young woman who takes upon herself to seek revenge for her best friend who committed suicide after being raped by their classmate. For what I remembered, I was very uncomfortable watching this movie. Cassie, the main character, was in a very vulnerable state. She would go to clubs and be almost raped by any guy she met in the bar. This shows how women are always vulnerable unless society intervenes.

Barakamon 2023

This is a live action series based on the Japanese manga "Barakamon" ばらかもん. Seishuu Handa is a an rising calligrapher: young, handsome and talented. When a veteran lables his piece as unoriginal, he completely lose his composure with severe repercussion. As punishment, his father exiles him to the Goto Islands where he meets boisterous children, fujoshi middle schoolers and energetic elderly. Same as the anime, it is a very refreshing series.
